#  HR Analytics Dashboard – Employee Attrition Analysis

## Overview

This project presents an interactive **HR Analytics Dashboard** built in Power BI to analyze employee attrition patterns and uncover key factors influencing workforce turnover.

The objective was to convert raw HR data into actionable business insights that support data-driven decision-making in HR strategy.

---

## Problem Statement

Employee attrition leads to:

* Increased hiring and training costs
* Productivity loss
* Organizational instability
* Reduced employee morale

This dashboard answers critical HR questions:

* Which departments have the highest attrition?
* Which employee segments are most at risk?
* How do salary, tenure, and job satisfaction impact attrition?
* What patterns exist across age groups and experience levels?

---

## Key KPIs Tracked

* Total Employees
* Attrition Count
* Attrition Rate (%)
* Average Age
* Average Salary
* Average Years at Company

---

## Insights Derived

*  Higher attrition observed among employees with 1–3 years of tenure
*  Overtime and low job satisfaction strongly correlate with attrition
*  Certain departments show significantly higher exit rates
*  Lower salary slabs experience comparatively higher attrition
*  Younger workforce segments show higher mobility trends

These insights can help HR teams proactively design retention strategies.
